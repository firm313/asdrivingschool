Getting a Permit

Documents Required

  • Original birth certificate or US passport (expired not more than 3 years)
  • High School waiver card (blue squared card)
  • Social Security card, copy or ITIN Number
  • Proof of address

Teen Special

  • Documents must be either dropped off at our office or pick up may be arranged by appointment.
  • Vision test
  • 3 sessions of 2 hours each behind‑the‑wheel instruction for 6 hours in total. 1 to 1 teaching, permit validation (includes road test scheduling) and insurance certificate.
  • Road test is NOT included in this package
